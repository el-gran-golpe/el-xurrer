In the heart of Lyon, Laura Vigne walks barefoot, drawn by the whispers of a legend older than the city itself: Les Empreintes, a mysterious relic said to hold the power of the earth and the secrets of mankind’s deepest connection to it.  

No one has ever seen it, yet its presence pulls at her with every step she takes.  
As she navigates the struggles of a world unraveling, Claire’s path becomes intertwined with this elusive object, a symbol of hope and mystery.  

Little does she know, Les Empreintes holds a truth not of magic, but of humanity’s forgotten past.  
The key to survival lies not in finding it, but in understanding it—a secret woven through time, waiting to be uncovered piece by piece.
